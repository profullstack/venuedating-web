Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds at barcrush ("we," "our," or "us"). We strive to provide high-quality document conversion services and ensure customer satisfaction. This policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our business.

2. Subscription Refunds

We offer the following refund options for our subscription services:

2.1 Cancellation Within 14 Days

If you cancel your subscription within 14 days of the initial purchase (the "cooling-off period"), you are eligible for a full refund of your subscription fee. This cooling-off period gives you time to evaluate our services and determine if they meet your needs.

2.2 Cancellation After 14 Days

If you cancel your subscription after the 14-day cooling-off period, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and no refund will be provided for the remainder of that period. You will not be charged for subsequent billing cycles.

3. Service Disruptions

In the event of significant service disruptions or downtime that materially affects your ability to use our services, we may, at our discretion, offer:

  • A partial refund proportional to the duration of the disruption
  • Service credits for future use
  • An extension of your subscription period

The specific remedy will depend on the nature, duration, and severity of the disruption.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@barcrush.app with the following information:

  • Your account email address
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the refund request

We will process valid refund requests within 5-10 business days. Refunds will be issued using the original payment method used for the purchase.

5. Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in the following circumstances:

  • The refund request is made after the 14-day cooling-off period
  • There is evidence of fraud or abuse
  • The service has been used extensively during the refund period
  • The account has violated our Terms of Service

6. Promotional Offers and Free Trials

Special promotional offers, discounts, and free trials may have different refund terms. These terms will be clearly communicated during the sign-up process for such offers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at support@barcrush.app.
